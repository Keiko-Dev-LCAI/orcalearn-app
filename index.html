❓ How to Use OrcaLearn

🐋 Welcome to OrcaLearn
OrcaLearn is an AI-powered homeschool helper. It generates full lesson plans and quizzes for any subject and grade level. All your data stays in your browser — nothing is stored on anyone else's computer. AI runs through the Lightchain network, not through us.
🚀 Getting Started
You get 5 free lessons and quizzes before needing to subscribe — no wallet required.

To start, click Continue Without Wallet on the home screen, or connect a MetaMask / Trust Wallet to unlock unlimited access for $4.99/month in LCAI.
👨‍👩‍👧 Setting Up Students
Go to the Students tab and tap + Add Student. Enter the child's name, pick a grade level, and choose an avatar. You can add as many students as you need.

On the Home tab, tap a student's name at the top to switch between children. All lessons and quiz scores are tracked separately per student.
📚 Generating a Lesson Plan
Go to the Lessons tab and tap + New Lesson. Choose a subject, grade, and how many days (1–10). You can add optional notes like "focus on fractions" or "keep it fun."

The AI generates a full multi-day plan in about 30 seconds. Tap any lesson card to open it and see the full plan broken into daily activities.

Check off each day as you complete it — OrcaLearn tracks your progress automatically.

Download / Print: Open a lesson and tap the blue Download button. A print-ready version opens in a new tab. Press Ctrl+P to print it, or change the printer to Save as PDF to keep a digital copy.
🎯 Taking a Quiz
Go to the Quiz tab. Pick a subject, grade level, number of questions, and set a time limit if you want one. For Math, you can also pick a specific topic like Addition, Fractions, or Word Problems.

Tap Start Quiz. Each question shows 4 choices — tap your answer. You'll see instantly if you got it right. At the end you'll see a score, grade (A–F), and full results saved to your history.
📊 Progress & Reports
The Home tab shows a summary of lessons completed and quizzes taken for the active student.

In the Students tab, tap a student and you'll see options to:
• Progress Report — a printable summary of all lessons and quiz scores
• Transcript — a formal academic transcript with GPA (great for college prep or record-keeping)
• Certificate — appears on a completed lesson; a printable certificate of completion
💳 Subscribing ($4.99/month)
After your 5 free uses, you'll see a payment prompt. Connect your MetaMask or Trust Wallet, then tap Pay Now. The app calculates the current LCAI equivalent of $4.99 automatically.

Send the LCAI from your wallet to complete the payment. Once confirmed on the blockchain, your access is unlocked for 30 days — no accounts, no passwords, no middleman.
🔒 Your Privacy
All student names, grades, lesson plans, and quiz scores are stored only in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server. Clearing your browser data will erase it, so use the Download button regularly to keep printed or PDF copies of important lesson plans.
